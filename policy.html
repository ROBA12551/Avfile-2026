1. Privacy Policy Overview

Avfile is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency about how we handle your data. This comprehensive Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our file sharing service. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other international data protection regulations.

Key Commitment: We respect your privacy and never sell your personal data to third parties without explicit consent.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our secure file sharing service:

2.1 Files & Content

  • Files you upload to our platform (stored securely)
  • File metadata (name, size, type, upload date)
  • Sharing preferences and access controls

2.2 Usage & Activity Data

  • Access logs and activity history
  • Download and sharing patterns (anonymized)
  • Platform usage statistics and performance metrics

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address and geographic location (for security)
  • Device information (browser, OS, device type)
  • Connection quality and transfer speed data

2.4 Optional Information

  • Email address (for account notifications)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Support requests and feedback

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data strictly for these legitimate purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Providing and maintaining the file sharing service
  • Processing file uploads and downloads
  • Managing sharing links and access permissions
  • Authenticating users and maintaining sessions

3.2 Security & Fraud Prevention

  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Identifying and blocking malicious content
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity patterns
  • Implementing abuse prevention measures

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Developing new features based on user feedback
  • Improving user experience and interface design
  • Conducting analytics and research

3.4 Legal Compliance

  • Complying with court orders and legal requests
  • Protecting against illegal activity
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Maintaining compliance with regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your files and personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4.1 Encryption Standards

  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transfers
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption for stored files (optional)
  • End-to-End: Optional client-side encryption available

4.2 Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • IP whitelisting for enterprise accounts
  • Session management and timeout controls

4.3 Infrastructure Security

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • DDoS protection and mitigation
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security patches and updates

4.4 Auditing & Compliance

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Annual SOC 2 Type II compliance certification
  • Comprehensive audit logs and activity tracking
  • Third-party security assessments
Security Disclaimer: While we implement comprehensive security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection against all threats.

5. File Retention & Deletion

5.1 Data Retention Period

  • User Files: Stored indefinitely until you delete them
  • Activity Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
  • Backups: Maintained for 30 days after deletion
  • Legal Records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

5.2 File Deletion

You can delete your files anytime through your account dashboard. Once deleted:

  • Files are immediately removed from active storage
  • Sharing links become inactive
  • Backup copies are deleted within 30 days
  • Complete deletion is confirmed within 48 hours

5.3 Automatic Deletion

Avfile reserves the right to automatically delete files that:

  • Violate our Terms of Service or applicable laws
  • Contain malware or harmful content
  • Remain inactive for 180 days (free accounts)
  • Are flagged as suspicious or illegal
