1. Privacy Policy Overview

Avfile is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

Key Commitment: We never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our secure, reliable, and anonymous file sharing service. Data collection is limited to what is required for functionality, security, and legal compliance.

2.1 Files & Content

  • Files you upload to the Avfile platform
  • File metadata such as file name, size, type, and upload timestamp
  • Access permissions, sharing settings, and optional password protection

Uploaded files are stored securely and are only accessible via the generated sharing link. We do not analyze or inspect file contents unless required for security or legal reasons.

2.2 Usage & Activity Data

  • Access logs related to file uploads and downloads
  • Download counts and access timestamps (anonymized)
  • System interaction data used to detect abuse or misuse

Usage data is primarily collected to maintain platform stability, prevent abuse, and improve overall performance. This data is aggregated and anonymized whenever possible.

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address (temporarily stored for security and fraud prevention)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device category
  • Connection data such as transfer speed and error diagnostics

Technical information helps us diagnose issues, protect against malicious activity, and optimize the service for different devices and regions.

2.4 Optional User-Provided Information

  • Email address (only if voluntarily provided for notifications)
  • User preferences and optional settings
  • Support requests, inquiries, and feedback communications

Providing optional information is not required to use Avfile. Anonymous file sharing remains fully available without registration or account creation.

Data Minimization Principle:
Avfile follows a strict data minimization policy. We collect only what is necessary to operate the service securely and efficiently, and nothing more.
